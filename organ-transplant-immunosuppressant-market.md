<p><p>The organ transplant immunosuppressant market comprises several key drug categories, including Tacrolimus, Mycophenolate Mofetil, Cyclosporin, and others. Tacrolimus is a potent calcineurin inhibitor crucial for preventing organ rejection. Mycophenolate Mofetil functions as an antimetabolite that impairs lymphocyte proliferation. Cyclosporin, another calcineurin inhibitor, suppresses immune responses to transplanted organs. Other agents may include azathioprine and everolimus, which offer alternative mechanisms to manage immunosuppression. Together, these medications are vital for improving transplant success and patient outcomes.</p></p>
