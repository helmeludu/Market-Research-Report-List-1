<p><p>Endpoint Privilege Manager (EPM) is a cybersecurity solution designed to manage and control the privileges of users and applications on endpoint devices. It enforces the principle of least privilege, allowing users to operate with minimal access required for their tasks, thereby reducing the risk of unauthorized access and potential breaches. EPM solutions provide visibility into user activity, limit permissions, and can automate privilege escalation requests, enhancing the overall security posture of organizations.</p><p>The Endpoint Privilege Manager Market is experiencing robust growth, driven by an increasing number of cyber threats and the need for organizations to secure their data across endpoints. Businesses are increasingly adopting EPM solutions to mitigate risks associated with insider threats and manage compliance with regulation standards. Emerging trends include integration with artificial intelligence and machine learning to enhance decision-making capabilities and automate threat detection. Additionally, the rise of remote work has underscored the importance of securing endpoints outside traditional network perimeters. The Endpoint Privilege Manager Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10% during the forecast period, reflecting the escalating recognition of endpoint security as a critical component of organizational strategy.</p></p>
