<p><strong>The Functional Dyspepsia Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prokinetic Drugs</li><li>Gastric Antisecretory Drugs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Functional Dyspepsia drug market includes two primary types: prokinetic drugs and gastric antisecretory drugs. Prokinetic drugs enhance gastrointestinal motility, aiding in the movement of food through the digestive tract, which can alleviate symptoms like bloating and nausea. Gastric antisecretory drugs, on the other hand, reduce stomach acid production, providing relief from discomfort caused by excessive acidity. Together, these drug classes target the underlying mechanisms of functional dyspepsia, helping to improve patient quality of life and manage symptoms effectively.</p></p>
