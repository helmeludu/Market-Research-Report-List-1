<p><strong>The Pickup Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pickup wheel speed sensor market comprises two primary types: Hall effect sensors and magnetic electric sensors. Hall effect sensors utilize a magnetic field to detect the speed and position of the wheels, providing accurate and reliable data for vehicle stability and traction control systems. In contrast, magnetic electric sensors generate voltage in response to changes in magnetic fields, allowing for speed measurements. Both types are crucial for enhancing vehicle safety and performance, catering to diverse automotive applications.</p></p>
