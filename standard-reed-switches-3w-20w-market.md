<p><p>Standard Reed Switches in the 3W-20W range are crucial components in various applications, distinguishing themselves by two main market types: Normally Open (NO) and Normally Closed (NC). Normally Open switches complete the circuit when actuated, making them ideal for sensors and alarms. In contrast, Normally Closed switches maintain the circuit until actuated, ensuring a continuous signal until interruption. Each type serves specific needs in automation, security systems, and industrial applications, enhancing functionality and efficiency in electronic devices.</p></p>
