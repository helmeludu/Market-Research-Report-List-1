<p><strong>The Biogas Upgrading Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The biogas upgrading market comprises several technologies for refining biogas into higher-quality biomethane. Membrane separation uses selective permeability to separate methane from impurities. Water scrubbing employs water to absorb carbon dioxide and other contaminants while leaving methane behind. Chemical scrubbing utilizes a chemical solvent to selectively remove unwanted gases. Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) utilizes adsorbent materials to capture impurities at high pressure, releasing purified methane when pressure is lowered. Other methods include cryogenic techniques and biofiltration, enhancing biogas's market viability.</p></p>
