VOx sensors provide higher sensitivity and better thermal resolution, making them ideal for applications requiring precise temperature measurements. In contrast, A-Si sensors are typically more cost-effective and offer decent performance for standard thermal imaging tasks. Both technologies cater to various industries, including security, military, automotive, and medical, driving the growth of the thermal imaging market by enhancing situational awareness and monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
