<p><ul><li>Aluminium Carbide</li><li>Calcium Carbide</li><li>Silicon Carbide</li><li>Tungsten Carbide</li><li>Iron Carbide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbide tools market encompasses various types, each serving distinct applications. Aluminium carbide is utilized in aluminum production and welding, while calcium carbide is essential for acetylene gas manufacturing. Silicon carbide is known for its hardness and thermal stability, making it ideal for abrasives and semiconductor applications. Tungsten carbide, highly valued for its strength, is widely used in cutting tools and wear-resistant applications. Iron carbide plays a role in steelmaking and as a hardening agent in various industrial processes.</p></p>
