<p><ul><li>PTFE</li><li>FEP</li><li>PFA/MFA</li><li>ETFE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-performance fluoropolymers market encompasses various types, including PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene), FEP (fluorinated ethylene propylene), PFA (perfluoroalkoxy alkane)/MFA (modified fluorinated alkylene), and ETFE (ethylene tetrafluoroethylene). PTFE is known for its exceptional chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, making it ideal for coatings and gaskets. FEP offers transparency and ease of processing, while PFA combines the benefits of PTFE with better flow characteristics. ETFE provides enhanced mechanical strength and is suitable for applications requiring durability in harsh environments, such as architectural films and insulation.</p></p>
