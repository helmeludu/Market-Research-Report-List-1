<p><p>The semiconductor vacuum valve market is categorized into three main types: Low Vacuum Valves, High Vacuum Valves, and Ultra High Vacuum Valves. Low Vacuum Valves operate at pressures above 1 mbar, suitable for applications requiring moderate vacuum levels. High Vacuum Valves function between 1 mbar and 10^-7 mbar, ideal for more demanding environments, often found in manufacturing processes. Ultra High Vacuum Valves, operating below 10^-7 mbar, are essential for critical applications in research and advanced semiconductor fabrication, ensuring optimal process purity and efficiency.</p></p>
