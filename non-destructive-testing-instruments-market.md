<p><p>The non-destructive testing (NDT) instruments market encompasses various types of equipment used to assess materials without causing damage. Ultrasonic test equipment employs high-frequency sound waves to detect flaws, while magnetic particle test equipment identifies surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials. Visual inspection equipment allows for direct observation of material conditions. Radiography test equipment uses X-rays or gamma rays for internal examination, and penetrant test equipment reveals surface discontinuities. Other instruments may include eddy current and thermographic systems, expanding diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
