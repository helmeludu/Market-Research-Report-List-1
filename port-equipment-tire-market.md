<p><p>The Port Equipment Tire Market consists of three primary types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, providing cushioning and better traction on uneven surfaces, ideal for various port operations. Solid tires are puncture-proof and offer durability, making them suitable for heavy machinery but at a cost of less shock absorption. Polyurethane tires combine flexibility and resilience, providing good wear resistance and reduced weight, making them increasingly popular for electric equipment in ports.</p></p>
