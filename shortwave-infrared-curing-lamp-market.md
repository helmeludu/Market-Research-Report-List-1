<p><p>The Shortwave Infrared Curing Lamp market can be divided into two main types based on orientation: lengthwise direction and lateral direction. Lengthwise direction lamps are designed for applications requiring a continuous, linear heating effect along long surfaces, ideal for curing large items. In contrast, lateral direction lamps are oriented to provide even heating across a wider area, making them suitable for curing coatings on surfaces that are broader or have varying dimensions. Both types cater to specific industrial curing needs.</p></p>
