<p><ul><li>On-board Electric Vehicle Charger (EVC)</li><li>Off-board Electric Vehicle Charger (EVC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Charger (EVC) market comprises two main types: on-board and off-board chargers. On-board chargers are integrated into the electric vehicle, converting AC power from external sources to the DC required for the vehicle's battery. Off-board chargers, on the other hand, are standalone units located outside the vehicle, supplying high-power charging directly to the battery, often found in public charging stations. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the accessibility and efficiency of electric vehicle charging infrastructure.</p></p>
