<p><strong>The MAD Oral Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One Piece Device</li><li>Two Piece Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MAD (Mandibular Advancement Device) oral device market is primarily divided into two types: one-piece and two-piece devices. One-piece devices, molded to fit the patient's mouth, offer simplicity and ease of use, making them suitable for general users. In contrast, two-piece devices consist of separate upper and lower trays connected by a hinge, allowing for more adjustable positioning of the jaw. This versatility often provides better comfort and efficacy, making two-piece devices popular for more severe cases of sleep apnea or snoring.</p></p>
