<p><p>Infrared microbolometer detectors are vital in thermal imaging applications, categorized primarily into three types: Vanadium Oxide (VOx), Amorphous Silicon (A-Si), and Others. VOx detectors utilize vanadium oxide as the sensing material, offering high sensitivity and performance in varying temperatures. A-Si detectors, made from amorphous silicon, are cost-effective and suitable for commercial applications. The "Others" category includes alternative materials and technologies that may be emerging, catering to specific market needs, performance requirements, or cost considerations.</p></p>
