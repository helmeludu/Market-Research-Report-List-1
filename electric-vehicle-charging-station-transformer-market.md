<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Charging Station Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-phase</li><li>Three-phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Vehicle Charging Station Transformers are categorized into single-phase and three-phase types based on their power supply characteristics. Single-phase transformers typically operate on a single alternating current circuit, providing charging for residential and low-power applications, making them suitable for home use or low-demand locations. In contrast, three-phase transformers handle a higher power capacity and are designed for commercial and high-traffic charging stations, enabling faster charging times and supporting multiple vehicles simultaneously. Each type serves distinct charging needs and infrastructure requirements.</p></p>
