<p><p>The bomb detection system market is categorized into three types based on the state of the substances detected: solid, liquid, and gas. Solid detection systems identify explosive materials in their solid form, often using X-ray or metal detection techniques. Liquid detection systems focus on identifying threats concealed in liquid containers, utilizing methods like spectroscopy. Gas detection systems target volatile substances and explosives in vapor form, employing technologies such as chemical sensors and infrared detection. Each type addresses specific challenges in ensuring safety and security.</p></p>
