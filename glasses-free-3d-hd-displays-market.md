<p><p>Glasses-free 3D HD displays use various technologies to create depth perception without special eyewear. Light barrier technology employs arrays of vertical barriers to direct light towards viewers, while lenticular lens technology uses lenses to manipulate light for 3D effects. Directional backlight involves illuminating pixels from specific angles to enhance three-dimensionality. Direct imaging uses holographic techniques for vivid depth, and other emerging technologies may include advances in micro-displays or parallax renderings, broadening the market's scope and applications.</p></p>
