<p><p>The isolation valves market comprises various types including ball valves, butterfly valves, knife gate valves, and plug valves, each serving distinct applications. Ball valves ensure quick shut-off with low flow resistance, making them ideal for on/off control. Butterfly valves provide space efficiency and are suitable for large flow volumes. Knife gate valves excel in handling slurries and thick fluids, while plug valves offer reliable sealing and easy operation. Other valve types complement these, serving specialized needs across industries.</p></p>
