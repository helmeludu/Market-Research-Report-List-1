<p><p>The heating pad market includes three main types: microwavable heating pads, electric heating pads, and chemical heating pads. Microwavable pads contain materials that retain heat after being heated in a microwave, providing portable relief. Electric heating pads use electrical power to generate heat, offering adjustable temperature settings for targeted therapy. Chemical heating pads utilize exothermic chemical reactions to produce warmth, convenient for on-the-go use without power. Each type caters to different preferences for pain relief and convenience.</p></p>
