Semi-intensive roofs combine moderate plant diversity and soil depth, allowing for more design flexibility and aesthetic appeal. Intensive roofs support a wider range of plant types, including trees and shrubs, requiring more maintenance and structural support, but offering diverse landscaping and recreational opportunities. Each type caters to different architectural and ecological needs.</p></p>
