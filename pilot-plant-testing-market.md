<p><ul><li>Ordinary Pilot Plant Testing</li><li>Continuous Pilot Plant Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pilot plant testing is crucial for validating processes before full-scale production. The ordinary pilot plant testing market focuses on batch processes, allowing for experimental runs to assess feasibility, optimize parameters, and scale up operations in a controlled environment. In contrast, the continuous pilot plant testing market emphasizes uninterrupted operation, simulating real-world conditions for processes that require constant flow. Both types enable industries to refine methodologies, reduce risks, and ensure efficient transfer from lab-scale to large-scale production.</p></p>
