<p><strong>The Oxygen Supply System to Hospitals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cylinder Manifold System</li><li>Liquid Oxygen System</li><li>Oxygen Concentrator System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oxygen supply system in hospitals includes three primary market types: </p><p>1. **Cylinder Manifold System** - Uses high-pressure cylinders interconnected to provide a continuous oxygen supply, suitable for short-term usage and emergency situations. </p><p>2. **Liquid Oxygen System** - Stores oxygen in liquid form, which is more space-efficient and provides a large volume of gas, suitable for larger facilities requiring a consistent supply. </p><p>3. **Oxygen Concentrator System** - Extracts oxygen from ambient air, enabling continuous, on-site oxygen production, ideal for smaller facilities or specific patient needs. Each system has unique advantages based on hospital size and patient care requirements.</p></p>
