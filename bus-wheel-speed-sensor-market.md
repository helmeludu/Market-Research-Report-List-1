<p><strong>The Bus Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bus Wheel Speed Sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel rotation, providing precise speed measurements essential for anti-lock braking systems and traction control. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors generate an electrical signal based on the wheel's movement, offering robust performance in various conditions. Both types are crucial for enhancing vehicle safety and efficiency, influencing their adoption in the growing bus manufacturing sector.</p></p>
