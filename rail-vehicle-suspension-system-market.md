<p><strong>The Rail Vehicle Suspension System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Primary Suspension"</li><li>"Secondary Suspension"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rail vehicle suspension system market comprises two main types: primary suspension and secondary suspension. Primary suspension is responsible for initial shock absorption, connecting the wheelsets to the car body, ensuring stability and comfort during operation. It primarily mitigates vertical forces. Secondary suspension, on the other hand, is located between the car body and the primary suspension, providing additional support and enhancing ride quality by dampening vibrations and lateral forces, thereby improving passenger comfort and the vehicle's overall performance.</p></p>
