<p><p>Magnetic encoders are essential in Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs) for precise position and movement tracking. Absolute magnetic encoders provide a unique position value at any moment, enabling reliable tracking over long distances without needing reference points. In contrast, incremental magnetic encoders measure movement by counting specific increments or pulses, making them ideal for applications requiring speed and direction monitoring. Both technologies enhance the navigation and operational efficiency of AGVs across various industrial and logistics applications.</p></p>
