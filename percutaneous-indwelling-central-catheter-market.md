<p><p>The percutaneous indwelling central catheter (PICC) market comprises two main types: Conventional PICC and Power-Injectable PICC. Conventional PICCs are primarily used for long-term intravenous therapy, offering stability and reduced infection risk. Power-Injectable PICCs are designed for procedures requiring high-velocity contrast agent injections, catering to diagnostic imaging needs.
