<p><strong>The PEX Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEX-A</li><li>PEX-B</li><li>PEX-C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PEX pipe market is categorized into three main types: PEX-A, PEX-B, and PEX-C. PEX-A offers superior flexibility and is ideal for tight bends due to cross-linking through the Engel method. PEX-B is more affordable, with excellent resistance to high temperatures and chemicals, produced via the silane method. PEX-C is known for its strength and is made using radiation cross-linking, offering a balance between flexibility and durability. Each type serves specific plumbing, heating, and cooling applications.</p></p>
