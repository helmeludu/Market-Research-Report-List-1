<p><p>E-payment markets can be categorized into cross-boundary and non-cross-boundary types. Cross-boundary markets involve transactions that span international borders, allowing consumers and businesses to engage in global commerce. This type often requires compliance with various regulations and currency exchanges. In contrast, non-cross-boundary markets are limited to domestic transactions, where payments and services remain within a single country's jurisdiction. These markets typically face fewer regulatory hurdles and often rely on local payment systems and currencies for seamless transactions.</p></p>
