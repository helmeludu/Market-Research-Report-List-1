<p><p>Password management software can be categorized into cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based password managers store credentials in the cloud, allowing users to access their information from any internet-connected device, promoting convenience and automatic updates. Conversely, on-premises solutions are installed locally on a user's device or company servers, offering enhanced control and security for sensitive data but requiring manual updates and maintenance. The choice between these options often depends on an organization’s security needs, regulatory requirements, and operational preferences.</p></p>
