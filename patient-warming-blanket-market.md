<p><p>The patient warming blanket market is segmented into three main types: full body, lower body, and upper body. Full body blankets provide comprehensive thermal coverage, ensuring the entire body is kept warm during medical procedures. Lower body blankets focus on heating the legs and feet, often used in surgeries where the upper body remains exposed. Upper body blankets are designed specifically for warming the chest, arms, and head, catering to procedures that require targeted warmth for enhanced comfort and recovery.</p></p>
