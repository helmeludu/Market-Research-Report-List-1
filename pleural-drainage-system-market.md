<p><strong>The Pleural Drainage System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transudative Pleural Effusions</li><li>Exudative Pleural Effusions</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pleural drainage system market is primarily categorized into two types based on the nature of pleural effusions: transudative and exudative. Transudative pleural effusions typically result from systemic conditions like heart failure or cirrhosis and are characterized by low protein content. In contrast, exudative pleural effusions arise from local factors such as infections, malignancies, or inflammatory diseases, containing higher protein levels. The distinction between these two types influences treatment approaches and the development of specific drainage systems in the healthcare market.</p></p>
