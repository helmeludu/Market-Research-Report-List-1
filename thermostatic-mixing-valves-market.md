<p><p>The thermostatic mixing valve market comprises two main types: Master Thermostatic Mixing Valves and Point of Use Thermostatic Mixing Valves. Master Thermostatic Mixing Valves are installed in larger plumbing systems to regulate the temperature of water throughout an entire facility, ensuring safety and efficiency. In contrast, Point of Use Thermostatic Mixing Valves are installed at specific outlets, such as sinks or showers, providing immediate temperature control for individual fixtures. Both types enhance comfort and prevent scalding in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
