<p><p>The metal heat treatment market encompasses various processes to alter the physical and sometimes chemical properties of metals, enhancing their performance and lifespan. General heat treatment includes processes like annealing, quenching, and tempering, which improve overall hardness, ductility, and strength. Surface heat treatment focuses on modifying the surface properties of metal components, such as hardening the surface while maintaining a softer core, commonly achieved through techniques like carburizing, nitriding, and induction hardening, to improve wear resistance and fatigue life.</p></p>
