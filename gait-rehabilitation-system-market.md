<p><p>The gait rehabilitation system market comprises two primary types: mechanical and computer-aided systems. Mechanical systems utilize physical devices like gait trainers and orthotics to support and improve a patient's walking abilities through structured exercises. In contrast, computer-aided systems leverage advanced technology, such as virtual reality and biofeedback, to create interactive rehabilitation environments, enhancing user engagement and tracking progress. Both types aim to optimize recovery and promote functional mobility in individuals with gait impairments.</p></p>
