<p><ul><li>Axial Expansion Joints</li><li>Angular Expansion Joints</li><li>Lateral Expansion Joints</li><li>Universal Expansion Joints</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The expansion joint market features several types, each serving specific functions. Axial expansion joints accommodate changes in pipe length along their axis. Angular expansion joints allow for angular movement between connected segments, accommodating misalignments. Lateral expansion joints enable lateral movements, compensating for horizontal displacements. Universal expansion joints can handle multiple types of movement simultaneously, making them versatile for various applications. Together, these joints ensure structural integrity, reduce stress, and enhance the longevity of piping systems in diverse industries.</p></p>
