<p><p>The soap powder market can be categorized into two main types based on phosphorus content: phosphorus and non-phosphorus soap powders. Phosphorus-containing soaps are effective in soil and stain removal but can contribute to water pollution through eutrophication. In contrast, non-phosphorus soap powders offer a more environmentally friendly option, minimizing ecological impact while still providing effective cleaning performance. As consumer awareness of sustainability grows, demand for non-phosphorus products is increasing, shaping trends in the soap powder market.</p></p>
