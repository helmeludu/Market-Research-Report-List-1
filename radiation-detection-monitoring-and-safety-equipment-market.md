<p><p>The radiation detection, monitoring, and safety equipment market comprises various technologies, primarily including gas-filled detectors, scintillators, and semiconductor-based detectors. Gas-filled detectors, like ionization chambers, measure radiation by ionizing gas within a chamber. Scintillators, which emit light when exposed to radiation, are used for their sensitivity and speed in detection. Semiconductor-based detectors rely on materials like silicon to directly convert radiation energy into electrical signals, providing high resolution and accuracy. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring safety and compliance in various industries.</p></p>
