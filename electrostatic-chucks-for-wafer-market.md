<p><strong>The Electrostatic Chucks for Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrostatic chucks (ESCs) are critical in semiconductor manufacturing for wafer handling, utilizing electrostatic forces to secure materials during processing. The Coulomb type electrostatic chuck employs attractive forces generated by applying a voltage, ensuring stable wafer positioning without mechanical clamps. In contrast, the Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type enhances performance by utilizing a combination of electrostatic forces and dielectric films, allowing for improved adhesion and precise control. Both types are essential for optimizing efficiency and yield in semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
